About Us
Our mission is to provide healthy customized meal for the inpatients with dietician’s guidance. We strive to empower healthcare providers and patients with knowledge and innovative solutions that foster health and recovery.
Expert Insights
At Pathiyam, we understand that each patient has unique dietary needs, and personalized nutrition plays a crucial role in recovery and overall well-being. Our team of experienced dieticians collaborates closely with healthcare providers to develop customized meal plans tailored to individual health conditions and preferences.
Commitment to quality
We prioritize the use of high-quality, locally sourced ingredients to prepare our meals. Our cooking facilities adhere to the highest standards of hygiene and cleanliness, ensuring that every meal served is not only nutritious but also safe. Our kitchens are equipped with state-of-the-art facilities and are maintained with rigorous hygiene protocols.
We are committed to providing meals that are both healthy and safe. Our stringent hygiene practices and dedication to using fresh ingredients set us apart in the realm of hospital nutrition.
